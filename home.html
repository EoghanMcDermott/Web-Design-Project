Welcome to UCD Softball!

UCD Softball Club play co-ed slowpitch softball throughout the academic year here on campus in UCD. We are a fun loving club that encourages fitness and social activity. Everyone is welcome! On this website you'll find info about when we train and the sport itself, as well as some nifty stuff from our social events.
